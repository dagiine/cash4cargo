Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Register an account, fill in your item details and delivery address, then confirm your order. You'll receive a tracking code via SMS once the order is placed.

Can I order from any Chinese website?

Yes — we support orders from Taobao, 1688, JD.com, Pinduoduo, and most other major Chinese e-commerce platforms. Just paste the product URL when creating your order.

What items are prohibited?

Prohibited items include flammable goods, lithium batteries over 100Wh, counterfeit products, narcotics, and live animals. Contact support if you're unsure about a specific item.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled before the item is picked up from the seller. Once in transit, cancellations are not possible. Contact support as soon as possible.

What are the estimated delivery times?

Express shipping takes 3–5 business days. Standard freight takes 10–15 business days. Times may vary during public holidays in China or Mongolia.

How do I track my shipment?

Enter your 6-character tracking code and phone number on our Track page. Real-time updates are provided as your cargo moves through our network hubs.

Where do I pick up my cargo in Mongolia?

We have warehouses in Ulaanbaatar (Zaisan, Bayanzürkh) and partner pickup points in major provincial centers. Your SMS notification will include the nearest location.

What happens if my package is delayed?

If your shipment exceeds the estimated delivery window by more than 3 days, contact our 24/7 support team. We will investigate and provide an updated ETA within 24 hours.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, QPay, SocialPay, and direct bank transfer. Corporate accounts can also request monthly invoicing.

When am I charged for my order?

Payment is collected after your items arrive at our China warehouse and the final weight and volume are confirmed. You'll receive an invoice before payment is processed.

Can I insure my high-value cargo?

Yes. Insurance can be added during checkout or by contacting support for an existing shipment. Coverage is calculated based on the declared item value.

Are there customs duties or taxes?

Customs duties apply for shipments over the duty-free threshold set by Mongolian customs. We provide duty estimates during checkout and handle all customs paperwork on your behalf.
